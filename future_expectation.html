The Future Expectation in India regarding Digitalization

Digital India is one of the greatest initiatives in the development of India. Till now it has been a great success. The future expectation is as follows:

  • Well defined infrastructure: High spees interent connection be available in every gram panchayat. Easy access to common service centre be available within their locality and safe and secure cyber space in the country
  • Governance and service on demand: A single window access be available to all the people by integrating departments of jurisdiction viability of government services on an online platform.
  • Digital empowerment of citizens: All digital services be universally accesible; all government documents and certificated be available on the cloud.
  • Cashless India: One major expectation is to move from cash-based transaction to digital transactions, which has already started gaining power
  • Online voting (E-election): Online voting systemwill be made possible by industry leading technologies. It shall lead to the following benefits:
    • It shall reduce the cost of printing, mailing, tabulating paper ballots and eliminates interlay from election process.
    • Election results shall be processed quickly.
    • No chances of booth capturing.

To make these come true, we must all together make an effort!
